Our Story

100+ YEARS OF
COMBINED INK

Satori Tattoo was born in 2018 when Chris — with 25 years of tattooing under his belt — moved to Colorado and opened shop on 4th Street within months. His wife Kim joined him soon after, and together they built something special in downtown Loveland.

They've since expanded to a larger studio just across the street, now home to 14 talented artists and piercers covering every style imaginable — fine line, traditional, realism, Japanese, neo-trad, illustrative, and more.

Satori Tattoo is a Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite across five Loveland and Fort Collins neighborhoods — and they've earned every bit of that love one satisfied client at a time.
